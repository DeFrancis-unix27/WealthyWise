How do I create an account on WealthPoint?

To create an account on WealthPoint:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage
  2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
  3. Verify your email address through the link sent to your inbox
  4. Complete your profile by adding personal information
  5. Set up your security preferences and two-factor authentication

Once completed, you can start adding your financial accounts and transactions.

Is my financial data secure with WealthPoint?

Yes, your financial data is extremely secure with WealthPoint. We employ:

  • Bank-level 256-bit encryption for all data transmission and storage
  • Multi-factor authentication options for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
  • Zero-knowledge encryption for sensitive financial information

We never store your banking credentials on our servers and use read-only access to your financial accounts.

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

To enable two-factor authentication (2FA):

  1. Click to your Account Settings from the Sidebar
  2. Click on "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  4. Enter the verification code from your authenticator app
  5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

Once enabled, you'll need both your password and a verification code from your authenticator app to log in.

How do I add a transaction manually?

To add a transaction manually:

  1. Navigate to the Transactions page from your dashboard
  2. Click the "Add Transaction" button
  3. Select the account for the transaction
  4. Choose between income or expense
  5. Enter the amount, date, and description
  6. Select a category (or let our AI categorize it for you)
  7. Add any notes or tags for better organization
  8. Click "Save Transaction"

Manual transactions will be included in your reports and analytics just like automated transactions.

Why isn't my transaction showing up immediately?

There are several reasons why transactions might not appear immediately:

  • Pending transactions: Most banks show transactions as "pending" for 1-3 business days before they clear
  • Sync timing: WealthPoint syncs with your financial institutions every 4-6 hours
  • Bank processing: Some transactions take longer to process, especially on weekends and holidays
  • Connection issues: Occasionally, temporary connection issues with your bank can delay syncing

If a transaction doesn't appear after 3 business days, please check your bank's website directly and contact support if the issue persists.
